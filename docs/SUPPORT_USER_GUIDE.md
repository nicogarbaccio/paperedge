## Accessing Support

### From the Sidebar

1. Click **"Support"** in the main sidebar (with the Help Circle icon)
2. You'll be taken to the Support & Help page

### From the Footer

1. Scroll to the bottom of the page
2. Click the **"Support"** link in the footer navigation

### Direct URL

Navigate directly to `/support` in your browser.

## Help Center

The Help Center provides comprehensive documentation on how to use PaperEdge.

### Browsing Help Articles

1. Click the **"Browse Help"** button on the Support page
2. A list of help articles will appear below the main cards
3. Articles are organized in 4 categories:
   - **Getting Started**: Learn the basics of PaperEdge
   - **Features**: Detailed guides for each feature
   - **Troubleshooting**: Solutions to common problems
   - **Advanced**: Tips for power users

### Searching for Help

**Using the Search Bar:**

1. Once the Help Center is open, you'll see a search box at the top
2. Type your question or keyword (e.g., "dashboard", "calculator", "notebooks")
3. Results will filter instantly as you type
4. Click on any article to read the full content

**Keyboard Shortcut:**

- Press **Ctrl+F** (Windows/Linux) or **Cmd+F** (Mac) to focus the search bar
- Start typing to search through articles

### Reading Articles

1. Click on any article title to expand it
2. The full content will display below
3. Click again to collapse the article
4. Use the **All** button to reset filters and see all articles

### Filtering by Category

1. Click any category button (Getting Started, Features, etc.)
2. Only articles in that category will display
3. You can combine category filter with search

## Reporting Bugs

Help us improve PaperEdge by reporting issues you encounter.

### Before You Report

- Check the Help Center to see if your issue is documented
- Ensure you can reproduce the problem consistently
- Note any specific steps that cause the issue

### Submitting a Bug Report

1. Click the **"Report Bug"** button on the Support page
2. A form will appear with the following fields:

   **Bug Title** (Required)
   - Brief description of what's not working
   - Example: "Button not clickable on dashboard"
   - Minimum 5 characters

   **Description** (Required)
   - Detailed information about the bug
   - Include steps to reproduce the issue
   - Explain what you expected to happen
   - Minimum 20 characters, maximum 2000

   **Severity Level**
   - **Low**: Minor issue, doesn't affect functionality
   - **Medium**: Noticeable issue, some impact on usage
   - **High**: Major issue, significant impact
   - **Critical**: App-breaking issue, prevents use

3. Fill out all required fields
4. Click **"Submit Bug Report"** to send

### Automatic Information Collected

The system automatically collects:
- Your browser type and version
- Operating system
- Screen resolution
- Timezone
- Language settings

**Note**: This info helps our team reproduce and fix bugs faster. We never share this data.

### Keyboard Shortcut

While filling the form, press **Ctrl+Enter** (Windows/Linux) or **Cmd+Enter** (Mac) to submit.

### Confirmation

You'll see a success message confirming your bug report was submitted. You can then close the form.

## Feature Requests

Suggest features you'd like to see and vote on requests from other users.

### Submitting a Feature Request

1. Click the **"Request Feature"** button on the Support page
2. The feature request section will expand
3. Click **"Suggest Feature"** to open the request form

**Feature Title** (Required)
- Clear, concise description of what you want
- Example: "Dark mode support"
- Minimum 5 characters, maximum 255

**Description** (Required)
- Explain what you want to achieve
- Describe how it would help you
- Minimum 20 characters, maximum 2000

**Priority Level**
- **Nice to Have**: Would be cool to have, but not essential
- **Important**: Would improve your workflow
- **Critical**: Essential for your use case

**Related Features** (Optional)
- List other features this relates to
- Helps connect similar requests

3. Click **"Submit Request"** to send
4. Your feature will appear in the community list with 0 votes initially

### Voting on Feature Requests

1. Open the feature request section
2. You'll see a list of all community suggestions
3. Each request shows:
   - **Title and Description** of the feature
   - **Thumbs Up button** with vote count
   - **Status**: Open, Planned, In Progress, or Completed
   - **Priority**: Nice to Have, Important, or Critical

4. Click the **Thumbs Up button** to vote on a feature
   - Button fills in to show you've voted
   - Vote count increases
5. Click again to remove your vote

### Why Vote?

- Popular features (with more votes) get prioritized for development
- Your vote shows us what you care about most
- Feature status updates as things progress

### Keyboard Shortcut

While filling a feature request form, press **Ctrl+Enter** (Windows/Linux) or **Cmd+Enter** (Mac) to submit.

## Mobile Usage

### Accessing Support on Mobile

The Support page is fully responsive and works great on phones and tablets.

### Differences on Mobile

- **Cards stack vertically** for easier scrolling
- **Text sizes adjust** for readability
- **Buttons are large** for easy tapping
- **Forms are optimized** for small screens

### Tips for Mobile

1. **Reporting bugs**: Use the landscape orientation for a larger form area
2. **Searching help**: The search bar is prominent and easy to find
3. **Voting on features**: Tap the thumbs-up button to vote
4. **Mobile keyboard**: The form hints work on mobile keyboards too

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is there a phone number to call for support?**
A: Currently, our main support channel is through bug reports and feature requests on this page. We review all submissions regularly.

**Q: How long does it take to get a response to a bug report?**
A: We aim to acknowledge critical bugs within 24 hours and other issues within 48 hours. You won't receive an email reply, but you can check the status of your reports in your account.

**Q: Can I delete my bug report after submitting?**
A: Contact our support team for assistance with modifying or deleting reports.

### Help Center

**Q: Why can't I find an answer to my question?**
A: Try different search terms, or submit a feature request for documentation on that topic. If it's a bug, submit a bug report instead.

**Q: Are there video tutorials?**
A: We don't currently have videos, but our Help Center articles are comprehensive. If you'd like video tutorials, submit a feature request!

### Feature Requests

**Q: Will my feature request definitely be built?**
A: Popular features are prioritized, but we can't guarantee every feature will be implemented. We focus on features that benefit the most users.

**Q: How often do you review feature requests?**
A: We review them daily. Features with the most votes are discussed first.

**Q: Can I vote multiple times on the same feature?**
A: No, you get one vote per feature. You can remove your vote and vote on a different feature if you change your mind.

### Bug Reports

**Q: Will my personal data be exposed?**
A: No. We only collect anonymized browser information (browser type, OS, etc.). We never collect personal data or account information.

**Q: What happens after I report a bug?**
A: Our team reviews bug reports and prioritizes them by severity. Critical bugs are fixed first. You can check the Help Center for workarounds in the meantime.

**Q: Should I report the same bug multiple times to get attention?**
A: No, please report it once with as much detail as possible. Multiple reports make it harder for us to track the issue.

### Keyboard Shortcuts

**Q: Why don't keyboard shortcuts work on my device?**
A: Keyboard shortcuts are primarily designed for desktop/laptop use. Mobile devices support the standard form submission methods.
